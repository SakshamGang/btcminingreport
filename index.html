Bitcoin Mining Pool Explanation

What is AntPool?

AntPool is one of the world's largest Bitcoin mining pools, operated by Bitmain since 2014. It aggregates computational power from multiple miners to increase the chances of mining blocks and earning rewards.

How It Works

  • • Miners contribute hashrate to collective pool
  • • Block rewards (3.125 BTC) distributed proportionally
  • • Higher success rate than solo mining

Network Difficulty Increases

Regulatory Changes

India's crypto regulations may evolve, potentially affecting mining operations.

Hardware may require repairs or replacement, affecting uptime and profitability.
